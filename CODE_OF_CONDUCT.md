# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Offer feedback that is actionable and specific.
- Be open to different viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberate disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it via:

- GitHub Security Advisories for sensitive issues
- Maintainer contact listed in the repository profile

All reports are reviewed promptly and handled with care.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans for repeated or severe violations.
